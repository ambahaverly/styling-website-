FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first session. Still have a question? Just get in touch.

Where do sessions take place? +

In-person sessions take place at luxury styling lounges including Harvey Nichols, Harrods, Selfridges and ME+EM, or at your home for wardrobe edits. Colour analysis and consultations can be held in Chelsea or via Zoom for online clients anywhere in the world.

What should I expect from my first session? +

Your first session begins with an in-depth conversation about your lifestyle, goals and current relationship with style. From there, we dive into the service you've booked — whether that's colour analysis, a wardrobe edit or an in-store session. Every step is relaxed, personal and completely focused on you.

Do you work with men as well as women? +

Absolutely. My services are fully gender-neutral and I work with clients of all genders. Whether you're looking for wardrobe clarity, colour guidance or a complete style overhaul — the approach is always tailored entirely to the individual.

What is colour analysis and do I really need it? +

Colour analysis identifies your unique seasonal palette from the 14 colour sub-seasons — the specific range of colours that make your complexion glow, your eyes brighter and your whole look more cohesive. Once you know your season, shopping becomes instinctive and getting dressed becomes effortless. It truly is the foundation of everything.

Can I book an online consultation from outside the UK? +

Yes — I work with clients worldwide via Zoom. The online style consultation and colour analysis (using digital drapes) are both available to clients anywhere in the world. For in-store styling, I'm also available to travel internationally for extended or bespoke packages.

How do I prepare for a wardrobe edit? +

No preparation needed — just make sure your wardrobe is accessible. I'll guide the entire process from the moment I arrive. It helps to have a rough sense of your lifestyle needs (workwear, casual, occasion) but even that isn't essential. We'll uncover everything together.
